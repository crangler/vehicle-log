# Summary: Vehicle Maintenance App
Date: 2026-05-01 | Ideas: 20 | Forks: 1

## Key Themes
- **Instant setup**: VIN/plate scan + odometer OCR → zero-typing onboarding
- **Smart scheduling**: dual triggers (miles OR time), component-level clocks, driving style modes
- **Multi-vehicle**: health score dashboard, family sharing, cost-per-vehicle graphs
- **Trust signals**: NHTSA recall auto-pull, TSBs, used car baseline mode
- **Long-term value**: parts logging, cost tracking, maintenance history PDF export

## Top Ideas (All 20)
1. Multi-vehicle garage with color/nickname/photo per car
2. Dual-trigger scheduling — miles OR months, whichever hits first
3. Component-level clocks — brake pads, battery, wipers each age independently
4. Vehicle health score — one glance tells you which car needs love most
5. "Conservative / manufacturer / extended" interval philosophy modes
6. Deferred service memory — skipped a rotation, app remembers and adjusts
7. Driving style adjustments — short trips, towing, dusty = shortened intervals
8. Three-question onboarding → instant pre-populated schedule
9. VIN scan — zero typing, auto-fills year/make/model/trim/engine
10. License plate scan — even easier alternative to VIN
11. Odometer photo / windshield sticker OCR — sets mileage baseline from a snap
12. Auto-pull open NHTSA recalls on day one
13. Auto-pull Technical Service Bulletins (TSBs) for your exact build
14. Animated reveal sequence — car "unwraps" itself during setup
15. Used car baseline mode — swipe cards to confirm/skip/unknown per service
16. Part-level logging — brand, part number, price, DIY vs. shop
17. Cost-over-time graph — which vehicle is actually costing more?
18. Maintenance history export PDF — clean handoff when selling
19. Seasonal reminders — winter tires in October, A/C check in April
20. Family sharing — spouse logs a service, you see it instantly
